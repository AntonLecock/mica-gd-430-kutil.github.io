HTML/CSS Chapters

In class, we started our adventures in HTML by looking at links. Specifically, Anchors ( <a> ), Images ( <img> ), CSS ( <link> ), and Javascript ( <script> ).

From the book HTML & CSS, take a look at the following chapters as a "refresher" for what we did in class.

  • Links - Chapter 4 - goes over the different ways you can link to something (like relative, absolute, and talks about the folder structure of our websites)
  • Images - Chapter 5 - Also talks about how to choose different file formats, which I forgot to go over in class. Very, very useful!
  • Chapter 1 - Structure - This covers some topics that we haven't gone over yet (like how HTML is a structured document), but also has a nice recap of what makes up an HTML tag.
  • Chapter 8 - Extra Markup - not sure why they call it "Extra Markup", but this goes over the important tags like <!doctype> , <html> , and <body> . It also talks a bit about the history of HTML. Good Stuff!
  • Chapter 10 - Intro to CSS - Refresher of the syntax for CSS, as well as some topics we haven't covered yet.
